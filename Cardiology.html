About Cardiology

Cardiology is the study and treatment of disorders of the heart and blood vessels. This includes diagnosis and treatment of congenital heart defects, coronary artery disease, heart failure, and other heart conditions.

Treatments

Common treatments include lifestyle changes, medications, and surgeries. Some specific treatments are:

  • Angioplasty
  • Coronary artery bypass surgery
  • Heart valve repair or replacement
  • Pacemakers and defibrillators
